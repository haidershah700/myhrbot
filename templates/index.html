About HR Policy Assistant

The HR Policy Assistant is an AI-powered chatbot designed to provide quick and accurate information about human resource policies and procedures.

Leveraging advanced language models and document retrieval technology, this assistant can answer your questions about:

  • Code of conduct
  • Recruitment and hiring processes
  • Compensation and benefits
  • Leave policies
  • Discipline and termination procedures
  • And much more!

Simply ask your question in natural language, and the assistant will provide the relevant information from your organization's HR documents.

How to Use the HR Policy Assistant
Asking Questions

Type your HR-related question in the input field and press Enter or click the send button. You can ask about any HR policy or procedure.

Using Suggestions

Click on the suggestion chips below the input field for quick access to common HR topics.

Customizing the Interface

Click the palette icon in the top right to customize colors, themes, and message styles.

Dark/Light Mode

Toggle between dark and light mode using the moon/sun icon in the top right.

Additional Features

Use the floating action button (+ icon) at the bottom right for options like:

  • Clearing the chat history
  • Exporting the conversation
  • Enabling reading mode
